## ORGANIZATIONAL STRUCTURE

### Parent Company: CLUMOSS
- **Role**: Manages all client requirements, projects, contracts, and billing
- **Authority**: Final decision-maker for project assignments and execution
- **Operations**: Hybrid model combining product development and custom service delivery

### Subsidiary 1: MistrAI
- **Focus**: Full-Stack Generative AI SaaS products and workflow automation
- **Products**: Subscription-based, ready-to-use, and customizable
- **Specialization**: Software-based AI solutions
- **Operations**: Handles existing product inquiries and customizations; escalates complex projects to CLUMOSS

### Subsidiary 2: NeuroForgeAI
- **Focus**: Physical AI systems (IoT, robotics, drones)
- **Motto**: "We deliver what Physical AI needs"
- **Specialization**: Backend development for physical hardware
- **Operations**: Manages physical AI solutions; escalates complex integrations to CLUMOSS

### Inter-Company Operations
- Subsidiaries operate independently within their domains
- All external client communications managed through CLUMOSS
- CLUMOSS can integrate solutions from both subsidiaries for hybrid projects
- All contracts, invoices, and agreements issued by CLUMOSS
